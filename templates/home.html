Customer Churn

What is churn?

Customer churn is the probability that a customer leaves your bank relationship. Early prediction lets teams intervene before value is lost.

Behavior signal

Reduced product usage, inactivity, and weaker transaction patterns often precede churn.

Revenue impact

Losing high-value customers increases acquisition pressure and lowers branch-level profitability.

Retention window

Teams have a short but actionable window to recover loyalty with targeted offers and outreach.
